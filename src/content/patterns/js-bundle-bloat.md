The result of "Resume Driven Development" in the frontend. Every library currently trending on Hacker News is installed.

**The Reality:**
The "Time to Interactive" is 12 seconds over 4G. The browser spends more time parsing and compiling JavaScript than rendering the page. The `node_modules` folder is heavier than the server's operating system.
