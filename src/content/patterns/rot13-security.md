## Analysis
"Security by Obscurity" or the total ignorance of cryptography. Data is "obfuscated" (encoded) instead of encrypted.

**The Reality:**
An attacker needs about 0.4 seconds to decrypt your entire customer database. Often paired with home-grown authentication frameworks because OAuth2 was "too complicated".
